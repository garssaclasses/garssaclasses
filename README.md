## 📖 About Garssa Classes

**Garssa Classes | Code to Career** is a modern educational initiative dedicated to helping students learn programming, develop real-world projects, master problem-solving skills, and build successful careers in technology.

Our goal is to bridge the gap between academic education and industry requirements through practical learning, mentorship, and career-focused training.

> **From Code to Career — Learn, Build, and Succeed.**

---

## 🎯 Our Mission

To empower students with technical knowledge, practical experience, and professional guidance that helps them become confident developers and industry-ready professionals.

---

## 🌟 Our Vision

To create a community where every student can access quality technical education and transform their passion for technology into a successful career.
